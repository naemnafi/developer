Q: Is a Precious Metals IRA risky?

A: All investments carry some level of risk. However, precious metals historically hold value over the long term, making them less volatile than some other assets.

Q: Can I take physical possession of my metals?

A: Yes, but only after you reach the eligible distribution age or liquidate the IRA. Until then, they must remain in an IRS-approved facility.

Q: Are there any tax penalties when transfering funds from my IRA to a Precious Metals IRA?

A: No. If you follow the proceedures, there are no taxes or penalties.

Q: What are the tax advantages?

A: Like other IRAs, a Precious Metals IRA offers tax-deferred growth for traditional accounts and tax-free growth for Roth accounts (if you meet all requirements).
